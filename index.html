🃏 Blackjack Rules

  • Try to get as close to 21 as possible.
  • Number cards are worth their face value.
  • J, Q and K are worth 10.
  • Ace is worth 11.
  • Click NEW CARD to draw another card.
  • If your total exceeds 21, you lose.
  • Exactly 21 is Blackjack!
